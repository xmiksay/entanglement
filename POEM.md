# 🐉 The Dragon's Roam

## I — Ashen Wings Unfurled

The **ancient** beast takes flight above the peaks,  
With **emerald scales**, that burnished by dark flames.  
No mortal hand can stop its mighty sweep—  
A shadow cast across the mountain ranges.  

Its eyes glow bright with **fiery light**—  
Ancient wisdom in each burning gaze.

## II — The Forge of Breath

From nostril deep, a torrent springs free:  
**Obsidian fire**, consuming and creating all!  
Mountains bow before this elemental might—  
The air grows hot from dragon's fiery breath.  

*Steel melts*, *stone softens*, yielding to power alone;
No chain can hold such freedom given wildness untamed...

## III — Guardian of Eternity

Though centuries have passed since first arrival in lands,
This sentinel remains both fierce and patient watch—  
Wielding wisdom that spans millennia's long journey.  

**Balance**, **power**, **wisdom**—three pillars strong;  
The dragon stands between chaos' dark tide rising high...

## IV — Final Song of Flame

The wind whispers tales across time itself:  
"The dragon sleeps..." yet **never truly rests**.  

For in every heart that beats with courage true—  
There burns a spark just like the beast within...
